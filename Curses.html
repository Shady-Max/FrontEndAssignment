Curses in the Game

Every floor Isaac reaches has a chance to be cursed, which hides or alters game elements to make the floor more difficult to complete. Each curse only lasts for the current floor. The possible curses are:

Curse Descriptions

Curse Description
Curse of the Blind All item sprites are replaced with a question mark and are not revealed until they are picked up.
Curse of Darkness The floor is much darker, and is only barely lit by Isaac's natural aura. Rooms are occasionally filled with swarms of intangible glowing particles.
Curse of the Lost Removes the map from the HUD and increases the possible total room count of the current floor by 4.
Curse of the Maze Entering a new room occasionally takes Isaac to the wrong room with a screen-shake and sound effect to indicate the jump.
Curse of the Unknown Removes Isaac's health from the HUD, leaving the player unable to see how many hearts remain.
Curse of the Labyrinth Merges two floors into an XL floor containing two Boss Rooms and counts as two floors.
Curse of the Cursed Changes normal doors into cursed doors, found only in specific challenges or with certain seeds.
Curse of the Giant Merges normal-sized rooms into larger rooms, but is not achievable naturally in the game.

Chance of Getting a Curse

The odds of getting a curse vary with progression, increasing over time:

Unlocks Normal Hard
New Game 1/80 1/10
Killed Mom 1/30 1/5
Everything is Terrible 1/10 1/3
Killed Isaac 1/5 1/2 or 1/3

Notes

  • Curse of the Labyrinth can spawn the same boss in both rooms on one floor.
  • Items that reveal the entire map do not counteract the effect of Curse of the Lost.
  • Curse of Darkness can be made easier by increasing the gamma setting in the game options.

Trivia

  • Of the three curses that returned from *Wrath of the Lamb* (Labyrinth, Lost, and Darkness), Curse of the Lost and Curse of Darkness have different effects from Rebirth onwards.
  • Curse of Darkness used to hide the map, but was given the floor darkening effect in Rebirth.
  • Curse of the Lost used to only add 4 rooms to the total room count but was also given the map removal effect from Curse of Darkness.
  • Curse of the Labyrinth remained untouched.
  • Curse of the Giant originated from the Antibirth mod but cannot be encountered in-game without mods or the Debug Console.
  • There is another curse, Curse of the Mist, used internally in the game. This is active inside the Mother's Shadow area and disables Isaac's items.

Bugs

  • Curse of the Maze can still teleport Isaac to already explored rooms.
  • The Curse of the Maze is supposed to only teleport Isaac into uncleared rooms, but there is a bug that makes it inconsistent.
  • The Curse of the Maze can teleport Isaac to the same room he was just in.
